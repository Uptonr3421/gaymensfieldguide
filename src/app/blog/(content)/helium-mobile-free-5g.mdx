## MIXTURE OF EXPERTS: The Brains Behind the Brains

Let's talk about Mixture of Experts (MoE). In essence, MoE is an AI architecture that divides a model into multiple specialized sub-networks, called "experts." A "gating network" then decides which experts are best suited for a particular task. This allows for greater efficiency and scalability. We here at Vibe Coder are firm believers in the power of MoE. It's like having a team of mini-brains working together to solve complex problems.

## Fun History Section

Did you know that the concept of Mixture of Experts was first introduced in 1991? That's right, Robert Jacobs and Geoffrey Hinton proposed dividing tasks among smaller, specialized networks way back then. Talk about being ahead of the curve!
