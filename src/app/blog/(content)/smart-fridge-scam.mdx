## Conclusion: Segregate or Die

If you must have a smart fridge (why?), treat it like a virus.
*   **VLANs:** Put IoT stuff on a separate Guest Network.
*   **Firewall:** Block them from the internet (they only need LAN).
*   **Dumb Devices:** Buy a normal fridge. Write a shopping list on paper.

The future is dumb. Stay unconnected.
