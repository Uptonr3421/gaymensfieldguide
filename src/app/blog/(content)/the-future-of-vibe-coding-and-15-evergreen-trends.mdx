## What is Vibe Coding?

It is the shift from **Instruction** to **Intention**.

You don't tell the computer *how* to loop through an array. You tell it *why* you are looping. You give it the "vibe" of the feature, and it handles the boilerplate.

## The 15 Evergreen Trends

Trends change, but human psychology is forever. "Vibe Coding" accesses these evergreen desires.

1.  **Status:** People want to feel important (VIP Tiers).
2.  **Fear:** FOMO is eternal (Countdown Timers).
3.  **Lazy:** One-click everything (Auth).
4.  **Novelty:** New shiny things (AI wrappers).
5.  **Tribalism:** Us vs Them (Android vs iOS).
