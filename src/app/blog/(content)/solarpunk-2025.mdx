## We Are Stuck in *Blade Runner*

Look at your setup. RGB lights. Black metal. Dark Mode everything.

We have aesthetically accepted that the future is dark, industrial, and oppressive. We dress our tech like weapons.

**Solarpunk** asks: What if the future was actually... nice?

## Technology as a Gardener

Solarpunk isn't about being a Luddite. It loves tech.

But it views tech as a **Grid**, not a **Master**.

## The "Green High Tech" Aesthetic

It's already happening.

*   **Framework Laptops:** Modular, repairable.
*   **Vertical Farming:** AI controlling hydroponics.
*   **Bio-Materials:** Mycelium packaging instead of Styrofoam.

This isn't hippie fantasy. It's engineering efficiency. Nature is efficient. The concrete jungle is not.
